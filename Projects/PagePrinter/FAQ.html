Frequently Asked Questions!

  • What Route Codes Are Supported?
    • Currently only CV, CX, MX Routes Are Supported in This Version.

  • Are CV Routes a Required Field?
    • CV Routes are Required During Cycle 1 When CV, CX Routes are put together in The Picksheets.
    • CV Routes are NOT Required During Cycle 2 Due to There Only Being MX Routes.
      • The CV Field May be Left Blank or Set to Zero and The Program Will Function.

  • What are Introduction Pages?
    • Introduction Pages are First Page/s Before The Pick Sheets are Listed.
      • The Default Number of Introduction Pages is 1.
      • If There is No Introduction Pages, List "0"

  • If There is Something You Would Like to Request, Chime "ULLAMAS"
